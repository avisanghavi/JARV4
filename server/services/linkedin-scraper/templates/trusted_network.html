Manage Your Trusted Network List (TNL)

Your Trusted Network List (TNL) is a group of your most trusted LinkedIn connections who you can rely on for warm introductions. iClout will prioritize leads that can be reached through these trusted contacts.

CSV should have columns for names, trust scores (1-10), and optional notes. We'll automatically detect column names.
CSV Format Tips:
  • Column headers like "name", "contact", or "full name" will be recognized as contact names
  • Columns with "trust", "score", or "rating" in the name will be used for trust scores
  • Columns with "note", "comment", or "description" will be used for notes
  • Trust scores should be between 1-10 (values outside this range will be adjusted)
  • The system supports comma, semicolon, and tab-separated CSV files
CSV Format Example:
name,trust_score,notes
Jane Smith,10,Former colleague at Acme Corp
John Doe,8,College friend
Michael Johnson,7,Industry contact
